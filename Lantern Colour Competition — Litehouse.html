How to enter

Step-by-Step

Here's exactly how to put your entry together. No design experience needed — just your ideas and Gemini.

1

Download a lantern image

Use this clean white-background product shot — it's the best image for AI recolouring. Download the lantern image here →

2

Open Gemini in Google Workspace

Go to gemini.google.com — you already have access through your Litehouse Google account. Upload the lantern image and use the suggested prompt below.

3

Generate your colour render

Use the prompt template on this page, swapping in your chosen colour. Iterate until you're happy with the result. You can submit up to 2 colours.

4

Prepare your pitch

Put together a short slide or a few notes explaining: What colour did you choose? Why will it sell? Who's the customer? You'll present live to the team.

5

Present on Wednesday 22 April

Share your render and pitch live with the team. Keep it short and sharp — think 2–3 minutes per entry. The team votes, and the best colour wins.

AI prompt guide

Your Gemini Starter Prompt

Copy this, upload your lantern image, and swap in your colour. Tweak until the render looks great.

"I'm uploading a product photo of a metal outdoor solar lantern on a white background. Please recolour the entire lantern body to [your colour here — e.g. sage green / terracotta / midnight navy]. Keep the metal texture, proportions, and all structural details exactly as they are. The background should remain clean white. Make it look like a premium product render — photorealistic and ready for an e-commerce listing."

Tip: If the first result isn't right, add "keep the original shape and structure unchanged" or specify the exact shade (e.g. "warm terracotta, like burnt clay").
